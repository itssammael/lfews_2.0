Introduction

Welcome to the LFEWS 2.0 User Manual. This platform is a critical infrastructure component designed to safeguard communities by providing real-time intelligence on environmental conditions.

LFEWS integrates advanced sensor networks with intuitive visualization tools to deliver a unified view of flood monitoring, weather patterns, and tidal movements, enabling data-driven disaster risk reduction.

02

Getting Started

Access & Login

Navigate to the application URL provided by your IT department. Enter your assigned email and password to securely enter the system.

Role-Based Access

Features are restricted based on roles (e.g., Administrator, Reviewer). If a module is missing, check your permissions with your administrator.
